Welcome to DIG-IT

We are team of talented high school students who seek to spread AI awareness and education across the world

Get Started

The Mission

AI is transforming our lives in profound ways and having basic AI skills, at varying levels, will soon be as imperative as having math skills. We fulfill this demand through integrating school level clubs that allow kids to come together and learn from each other and teach the under. This platform will provide curated content, which the students may themselves audit, that can help students ramp up quickly along with making AI/ML education available to all especially underprivileged children.

Objective

A platform where learning can be through interactions at clubs on schools and on the platform. Its objective is to bridge the gap between students from high-school and underprivileged children and raise funds for continued learning provided to underprivileged children at Non-Profits

Motto

People who learn (high-schoolers) are the ones who teach (those who are underprivileged).

Idea Inspiration

However, teaching AI at Indian schools has a long way to go. I know it from personal experience as a current high schooler scored full marks in CBSE X Term 1. I owe much of my AI knowledge to my own efforts in learning from the internet, my parents, other schoolmates and generally fiddling with various software built by the open-source community.

Covid has created enormous hurdles in learning, at varying degrees. Some kids were privileged to be part of private schools and could continue their education online, even though not with the same efficacy. Other kids completely lost it as their schools were not even equipped to impart online education. This platform can make AI/ML education available to all and help level the playing field. Partnerships with NGOs, Govt schools and CSRs can help achieve that goal to a great extent

Market Oppurtunity

Market – Education Industry (AI/ML for High-schoolers and Underprivileged children) Niche – High schoolers for High-schoolers (Kids would be learning through collaboration. They will create the content and also teach underprivileged kids. The Total Addressable Market is 3,35,000. About 3 lakh+ students in schools and 25,000+ underprivileged children in 500+ NGOs.

Minimum Viable Product

1. A team of 10-15 Students from my school AIS Noida participate in creating content and engaging with users and teaching kids at SADRAG NGO . It will be followed by reaching out to other schools and companies for sponsorships.

2. Web Platform dig-it.world -This platform will host all the content required for training, including code snippets, videos, instructions etc. It will also have section for Q&A and discussion forum
